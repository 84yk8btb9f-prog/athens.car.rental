Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a vehicle?

You can book via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or by filling out the contact form above. We'll confirm your reservation and provide all the details you need.

What documents do I need?

You'll need a valid driver's license (held for at least 1 year), passport or ID, and a credit card for the security deposit.

Is insurance included?

Yes! All our vehicles come with comprehensive insurance coverage. You can drive with complete peace of mind.

Can I deliver the car to the airport?

Absolutely! We offer airport pick-up and drop-off services. Just let us know your flight details when booking.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before pick-up. For cancellations within 48 hours, a small fee may apply. Contact us for details.

Do you offer long-term rentals?

Yes! We offer special rates for weekly and monthly rentals. Contact us to discuss your needs and get a custom quote.
